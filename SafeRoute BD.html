About the Project

This project aims to enhance road safety in Bangladesh by leveraging machine learning to predict the likelihood of traffic accidents. By analyzing various factors such as weather, road conditions, and time of day, our predictive model provides a risk assessment to help drivers make more informed decisions.

The system was trained on a comprehensive dataset of traffic incidents. We evaluated several machine learning algorithms to ensure the highest accuracy, including K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), Decision Tree, Random Forest, and Support Vector Machine (SVM).

Our goal is to create a practical tool that can be used by anyone to promote safer driving habits across the country.

How It Works in 3 Simple Steps

1. Provide Trip Details

Enter the conditions of your planned journey into the form above. Include details like the weather, road type, time of day, and your driving experience.

2. AI Analysis

Our trained machine learning model instantly analyzes your inputs, comparing them against thousands of historical accident records to identify patterns and risk factors.

3. Receive Your Risk Score

The model calculates a risk probability, which we translate into a simple, easy-to-understand rating: Low, Moderate, or High, helping you stay aware on the road.

Disclaimer: This tool is for informational purposes only and provides a prediction based on historical data. It is not a guarantee of safety. Always drive with caution, adhere to traffic laws, and be aware of your surroundings, regardless of the prediction.
